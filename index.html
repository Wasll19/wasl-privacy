Privacy Policy for Wasl (وصل)

Effective date: April 17, 2026

Wasl ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices when using the Wasl mobile application.

1) Information We Collect

  • Microphone access (RECORD_AUDIO): Wasl requests microphone permission only to support voice calling features within the app.
  • Account and usage data: We may process basic account details and technical logs required to operate, secure, and improve the service.

2) How We Use Microphone Data

  • Microphone access is used solely for real-time voice communication during calls initiated by you.
  • Wasl does not use microphone input for advertising purposes.
  • Wasl does not access the microphone when you are not using the calling feature.

3) Audio Recording and Storage

By default, Wasl uses microphone input for live call transmission only. We do not store call audio on our servers unless a specific recording feature is clearly provided to you and you actively enable it.

4) Permissions and Your Choices

  • You can grant or deny microphone permission from your device settings at any time.
  • If microphone permission is denied, voice calling may not function correctly.

5) Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited data with service providers only as needed to run core app functionality (such as hosting, security, or communication infrastructure), and only under appropriate confidentiality and data protection obligations.

6) Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

7) Children's Privacy

Wasl is not intended for children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate legal basis.

8) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version with a new effective date on this page.
